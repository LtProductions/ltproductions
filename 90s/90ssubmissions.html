HOW TO SUBMIT A MOVIE TO THE SITE:

    1. Make the movie (ofc, so that it can be submitted) [.vmm and .3mm are fine for this]

    2. If you have unused voice lines, whenever you quit the program and you get this,

    Click on the first option.

    3. (FOR .VMM FILES) If there are any unused mods in the movie, download Jayrod's VMM Cleaner to get rid of unused .vxp files in the film.

    4. Afterwards, pack the .vmm or .3mm file into a zip (if your movie has a custom font used for it, pack the font file with the movie) and upload it to mega.nz or mediafire.

    5. Send the link over to ltproductions1995@gmail.com with a thumbnail at the dimentions of 544x306.

    6. Whenever your submission is approved, you'll see it on The Community Film Page.
